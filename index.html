About me

Most stars will go through many more than 5 main stages however whenever certain stars have a mass large enough the star is considered a supergiant and has a violnetly short life. Massive stars evolve in a similar way to a small stars until it reaches its main sequence stage. The stars shine steadily until the hydrogen has fused to form helium ( it takes billions of years in a small star, but only millions in a massive star).
